HR-Data-Analysis – Report 

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of employee data to help the HR department make data-driven decisions related to workforce planning, promotions, and retrenchments. The dashboard is divided into three interactive pages — Home, Action, and Details — for better clarity and insights.

The Home page presents an overall summary of the workforce with key KPIs such as total employees (1,470), gender ratio (60% male, 40% female), employees on service (92%), and retrenchment rate (8%). It also displays insights on promotion eligibility, service years, job levels, and employee distance status.

The Action page identifies specific employees who are due for promotion (72) and those who may be retrenching (117), offering HR teams a clear list for follow-up actions.

The Details page gives deeper insights by department and job role. It highlights that Research & Development and Sales departments face the highest retrenchment and promotion activity. Employees with high job satisfaction and overtime contributions tend to perform better, reflected in an 84.63% high-performance rating.

Overall, this dashboard effectively transforms raw HR data into meaningful visuals, enabling quick and informed decision-making regarding employee performance, retention, and workforce balance.
